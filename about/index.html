WE WORK HARD TO MAKE VIRALBREATH THE WORLD’S MOST RESPECTED SOCIAL MEDIA AGENCY.

We’re ready to re-write the rules when it comes to social media and it starts with focusing on what really matters…content. Channels and technologies evolve, change, come and go, but great content stands the test of time. If you can effectively tell your company or organization’s story across all your channels and with the right people, well, that’s marketing. Unfortunately many companies and organizations don’t have the time, money or expertise to pull it off. That’s where we hope Viral Breath can fill the gap.

Viral Breath is about providing an agency solution that doesn’t feel like an agency solution. We work exclusively with small and medium sized businesses and we do so affordably. Big fancy downtown office? Nope. Expensive, over-sized staff? Nah. Streamlined, results-oriented, experience at your disposal? You bet. Our team’s collective years of experience have worked towards building Viral Breath by gaining a wealth of experience, a network of subject matter experts, and a business model that just plain works. No matter what the scope is of your specific marketing needs, Viral Breath can help create, curate and connect your story to the world.
