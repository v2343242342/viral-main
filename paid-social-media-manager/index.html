Paid Social Media Manager

Location: Remote
Status: Part Time

Viral Breath, a startup social media marketing agency is actively seeking a part-time Paid Social Media Manager to help with the management of some of our client’s paid media efforts. The ideal candidate will have experience managing social media advertising and strategy across the majority of all major platforms. We are a newly formed marketing agency and growing fast, and we’re looking for someone that is passionate about social media, eager to learn, grow and potentially be an integral part of the growth of our company. Ideally you are someone not needing a full-time opportunity (yet), but open to growing into one.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage the social media advertising efforts of assigned client(s)
  • Assist in the advertising strategy development for new and existing clients
  • Create and curate the necessary amount of ads for clients on a month to month basis
  • Oversee advertisement implementation for all assigned clients
  • Consistently meet or exceed set KPI’s for clients
  • Create and deliver weekly, bi-weekly and monthly reports for clients
  • Manage calls with clients to discuss results

    Required Skills

    • Experience in start-to-end social media advertising campaign development 
    • In-depth knowledge of Facebook and other social media or paid advertising platforms
    • Passionate about data, analytics and reporting
    • Efficient and effective time-management skills
    • Experience with various social media tools such as HootSuite, Sprout Social, or other.
    • Must have strong verbal & written communications skills, as well as an ability to present ideas logically & clearly 
    • Positive, team-oriented and the drive to be a high performer

    Terms

    • This is a part time, month to month position, 10-20 hours a week to start with the potential to grow into more
    • Independent contractor position (1099)
    • Pay is commensurate with experience on an hourly basis
    • This is a remote opportunity, you can work from anywhere
    • You will need to be accessible to the team and clients during business hours.
    • You will need your own computer/devices
