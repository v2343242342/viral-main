we care every month

We believe that there has to be more to our agency then just delivering social media results, and that more is Viral Breath Cares. Every month we highlight one of our team or client’s favorite cause or charity through a blog post, monthly social media posts, and a monetary donation. Our core values of Trust, Freedom, Innovation, Kindness and Generosity demand we do more, and we couldn’t be more thrilled that we are in a position to make a small difference in any way that we can. Have a cause or charity that you care about that you want us to highlight? Feel free to reach out and spread the love and we’ll consider featuring them!
